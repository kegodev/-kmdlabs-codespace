Commercial terms

Every project receives a written quote covering the final scope, responsibilities, timeline and deliverables.

Working together

Booking

A 50% deposit secures the project. Work starts once payment and the required content are received.

Balance

The remaining balance is due before final handover or domain transfer. Large builds may use payment milestones.

Revisions

Two revision rounds are included unless a package states otherwise. Additional work is charged at R450/hour.

Typical timeline

Launch page: 5-7 business days. Business site: 2-3 weeks. Store: 3-5 weeks. Custom systems: 4-8+ weeks.

Ownership

Final approved website files are handed over after full payment. Third-party services remain subject to their own terms.

Final quotation

Prices are guides. The approved written scope determines the final project price and what is included.

Separate third-party and content costs

Your KM Digital Labs fee covers the agreed design, development, setup and launch work. External providers bill their own recurring or usage-based charges, which may change independently. These costs are included only when the written quote explicitly says so.

  • Domain registration and renewal
  • Website hosting and storage
  • Premium themes, fonts or stock assets
  • Third-party software subscriptions
  • Payment gateway and transaction fees
  • Product photography and paid media
  • Copywriting and large content entry
  • Extra integrations outside the scope

All prices are in South African rand and act as a guide. Payment-provider approval remains subject to the provider's requirements. Final pricing follows the approved scope.
